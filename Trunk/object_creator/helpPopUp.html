Need Help Navigating the Canvas?

Adding Shapes
  • Select the color of your new shape by using the drop-down menu.
  • Select the shape that you wish to add.
  • Use the pop-up window to add the deminsions of your new shape.
  • Drag your new shape around the canvas by left-clicking on the object.

    • Uploading an Image
  • Click the "Choose File" button.
  • Select your image from your local files.

    • Free Draw
  • Click the "Free Draw" button.
  • Move your cursor to the canvas and click your mouse to draw

    • Manipulating Objects
  • To rotate, click the "Rotate" button, click on the object, then enter the degrees of rotation in the pop-up window.
  • To resize, click the "Scale" button, click on the object, then select an integer by which you want to increase you object. Negative integers will scale down the object.
